What Priyanka can do for you

Customized Meal Plans

You will be provided with customized, easy to follow and flexible diet plans. All these plans were designed according to your clinical profile, dietary profile, schedule, and food preferences. You will get constant unlimited guidance by our expert.

Lifestyle Modification

Majority of the metabolic disorders such as obesity, diabetes, cardiovascular disease, thyroid and other occurs due to disharmony in lifestyle factors such as unhealthy eating pattern, lack of physical exercises, lack of sleep, busy schedule, stress and alcohol or substance abuse. Here we use multifaceted approach. We will cover all these aspects of life.

Physical Exercises

Exercise is a fundamental basis for improving your physical health. It will not only promote strong muscles and bones but also, improves cardiovascular, respiratory, and overall health. Here we will give you a complete regimen for exercises and will facilitate in that too. Guidance will be given on types of exercises needs to be perform based on your clinical disease by our expert. You will get constant support.

Yoga

Yoga is the set of specific exercises, called poses, combined with specific breathing techniques and meditation principles. It is the oldest technique which is followed in India since ancient time and mentioned in Vedic scriptures. You will learn different yoga techniques to be performed on daily basis.
